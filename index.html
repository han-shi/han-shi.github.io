About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D student at Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) in Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. James T. Kwok starting from Sep 2018. Before my Ph.D journey, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University.

My current research interest is Automated Machine Learning (AutoML).
